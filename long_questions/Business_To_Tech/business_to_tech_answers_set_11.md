# Business to Tech Interview Questions & Answers - Set 11

## ðŸ”¹ 1. Technical Product Manager (Questions 1001-1012)

**Q1001: How do you measure if a product feature directly influences business revenue?**
* **Headline:** "Funnel Analysis."
* **Example:** "Did the feature increase the conversion rate at the Payment step?"

**Q1002: How do you identify latent customer needs that can create business opportunities?**
* **Headline:** "Observation."
* **Example:** "Users are hacking X to do Y. Let's build Y."

**Q1003: How do you decide between technical feasibility and strategic opportunity?**
* **Headline:** "R&D."
* **Example:** "High strategy, low feasibility? Invest in R&D to raise feasibility."

**Q1004: How do you integrate feedback from multiple markets into a single roadmap?**
* **Headline:** "Global Core."
* **Example:** "Build a 'Global Core' that works everywhere. Regions customize the edge."

**Q1005: How do you assess whether a product feature is mission-critical versus nice-to-have?**
* **Headline:** "Removal Test."
* **Example:** "If we remove it, does the user stop paying? Yes? Critical."

**Q1006: How do you ensure business metrics reflect technical delivery progress?**
* **Headline:** "Milestone Value."
* **Example:** "Milestone 1 delivers 20% of value. Not just '20% of code'."

**Q1007: How do you reconcile conflicting success metrics from different departments?**
* **Headline:** "CEO Arbiter."
* **Example:** "If Dept A conflicts with Dept B, CEO decides the priority."

**Q1008: How do you decide whether to invest in automation versus manual processes?**
* **Headline:** "Volume Math."
* **Example:** "Time to Automate > Time to do Manually * Volume? Don't automate."

**Q1009: How do you handle stakeholder pressure to accelerate delivery at the cost of quality?**
* **Headline:** "Debt Contract."
* **Example:** "We can ship fast, but we must allocate next sprint to fix the bugs. Deal?"

**Q1010: How do you ensure discovery insights are translated into actionable product backlog items?**
* **Headline:** "Insight Link."
* **Example:** "Link the Jira ticket to the User Interview clip."

**Q1011: How do you balance innovation against the need for stability in product releases?**
* **Headline:** "Stable Core."
* **Example:** "Don't innovate on the Login page. Innovate on the new Dashboard."

**Q1012: How do you identify which features will create competitive differentiation in the market?**
* **Headline:** "Kano Model."
* **Example:** "Delighters differ from Basic Needs."
