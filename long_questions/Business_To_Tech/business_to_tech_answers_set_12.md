## ðŸ”¹ 1. Technical Product Manager (Questions 1101-1112)

**Q1101: How do you decide which product metrics matter most for business impact?**
* **Headline:** "OMTM."
* **Example:** "One Metric That Matters. For Uber, it's 'Rides'. Everything else is secondary."

**Q1102: How do you balance feature innovation with maintaining platform stability?**
* **Headline:** "Budget."
* **Example:** "Error Budget. If errors > X, no innovation allowed."

**Q1103: How do you evaluate the business value of a new technical capability?**
* **Headline:** "Unlock."
* **Example:** "Does this allow us to sell to a new segment? E.g., 'Offline Mode' unlocks 'Rural Users'."

**Q1104: How do you prioritize between customer requests and strategic initiatives?**
* **Headline:** "Portfolio."
* **Example:** "Treat it like a stock portfolio. Mix of safe (requests) and risky (strategy)."

**Q1105: How do you manage stakeholder expectations when technical constraints exist?**
* **Headline:** "Early Warning."
* **Example:** "Tell them the constraint on Day 1, not Day 30."

**Q1106: How do you determine the right level of technical detail to share with executives?**
* **Headline:** "Stoplight."
* **Example:** "Red/Yellow/Green details. No SQL queries."

**Q1107: How do you decide when to sunset a feature that no longer drives business value?**
* **Headline:** "Zombie Check."
* **Example:** "If Usage < 1%, and it breaks often, kill it."

**Q1108: How do you measure whether new features drive desired customer behaviors?**
* **Headline:** "Cohort."
* **Example:** "Did the cohort who used the feature buy more?"

**Q1109: How do you handle conflicting KPIs across different business units?**
* **Headline:** "Optimization."
* **Example:** "Global Optimization > Local Optimization."

**Q1110: How do you ensure technical dependencies are accounted for in product planning?**
* **Headline:** "Pre-flight."
* **Example:** "Ask Lead Dev: 'What do we need before we start?'"

**Q1111: How do you balance user experience improvements with backend system complexity?**
* **Headline:** "Front-loading."
* **Example:** "Complex backend is okay if it makes Frontend simple."

**Q1112: How do you determine which technical experiments to scale into production?**
* **Headline:** "Win Rate."
* **Example:** "Scale the winners. Drop the losers."
