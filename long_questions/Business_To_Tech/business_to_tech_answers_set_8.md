# Business to Tech Interview Questions & Answers - Set 8

## ðŸ”¹ 1. Technical Product Manager (Questions 701-712)

**Q701: How do you determine if a product idea aligns with long-term company strategy?**
* **Headline:** "Strategy Filter."
* **Example:** "Company Strategy: 'Mobile First'. Idea: 'Desktop Widget'. Result: Reject. It fails the filter."

**Q702: How do you evaluate whether technical limitations should influence roadmap decisions?**
* **Headline:** "Feasibility Check."
* **Example:** "Roadmap: 'VR App'. Tech: 'Can't run in Browser'. Decision: Delay VR until Browser support improves."

**Q703: How do you prioritize competing feature requests from multiple stakeholders?**
* **Headline:** "Weighted Scorecard."
* **Example:** "Stakeholder A (Revenue) vs Stakeholder B (Support). Revenue request gets higher weight."

**Q704: How do you ensure measurable outcomes for product experiments?**
* **Headline:** "Hypothesis Statement."
* **Example:** "We believe X will cause Y. If Y increases by 5%, we persist. If not, we kill."

**Q705: How do you manage the balance between user experience and engineering constraints?**
* **Headline:** "Negotiation."
* **Example:** "UX wants 'Blur Effect'. Eng says 'Too slow'. Compromise: 'Simple Fade'."

**Q706: How do you make trade-offs when market opportunities conflict with technical capacity?**
* **Headline:** "Buy Capacity."
* **Example:** "Opportunity is huge. We have no devs. Decision: Hire Contractors immediately."

**Q707: How do you decide which product metrics truly reflect business value?**
* **Headline:** "Proxy vs Real."
* **Example:** "Clicks (Proxy) vs Purchases (Real). Optimize for Purchases."

**Q708: How do you validate assumptions before committing to product delivery?**
* **Headline:** "Pre-totyping."
* **Example:** "Sell the product before building it. If they buy, build it."

**Q709: How do you manage interdependencies between multiple products?**
* **Headline:** "Swimlanes."
* **Example:** "Product A release date aligned with Product B release date."

**Q710: How do you ensure your product team understands the strategic context of decisions?**
* **Headline:** "All Hands."
* **Example:** "I explain the Strategy every sprint planning."

**Q711: How do you handle evolving requirements after product launch?**
* **Headline:** "V2."
* **Example:** "Launch is done. Requirements change? That goes into V2."

**Q712: How do you communicate trade-offs between speed, quality, and scope to executives?**
* **Headline:** "Slider."
* **Example:** "You can have Fast and Cheap, but it won't be Good. Pick your slider position."

## ðŸ”¹ 2. Solutions Architect (Questions 713-724)

**Q713: How do you assess whether a proposed solution meets future scalability needs?**
* **Headline:** "Multiplier."
* **Example:** "Current load x 10. Does it break? If so, redesign."

**Q714: How do you ensure architecture supports both functional and non-functional requirements?**
* **Headline:** "Checklist."
* **Example:** "Functional: 'It works'. Non-functional: 'It works fast'. Check both."

**Q715: How do you handle legacy systems while implementing modern solutions?**
* **Headline:** "Wrapper."
* **Example:** "Wrap legacy in API. New system talks to API."

**Q716: How do you communicate technical risk to non-technical stakeholders?**
* **Headline:** "Traffic Light."
* **Example:** "Red = Stop (Danger). Yellow = Caution. Green = Go."

**Q717: How do you determine the right level of redundancy for critical systems?**
* **Headline:** "N+1."
* **Example:** "If one fails, we need one backup. N+1."

**Q718: How do you ensure architectural consistency across distributed teams?**
* **Headline:** "Reference Arch."
* **Example:** "Publish the 'Standard Architecture' diagram."

**Q719: How do you incorporate compliance and security requirements into architecture?**
* **Headline:** "Security by Design."
* **Example:** "Encryption is default. Not an option."

**Q720: How do you decide between open-source and proprietary technologies for a solution?**
* **Headline:** "TCO."
* **Example:** "Open Source is free license, high labor. Proprietary is cost license, low labor."

**Q721: How do you balance performance optimization against cost constraints?**
* **Headline:** "Diminishing Returns."
* **Example:** "Spending $10k to save 1ms is bad. Spending $10 to save 1s is good."

**Q722: How do you evaluate architectural trade-offs for multi-region deployments?**
* **Headline:** "Latency vs Complexity."
* **Example:** "Multi-region reduces latency but doubles complexity (Data sync). Is it worth it?"

**Q723: How do you ensure documentation is actionable and not just formal?**
* **Headline:** "Runbook."
* **Example:** "Docs must say 'If X happens, do Y'."

**Q724: How do you validate that architecture decisions align with business KPIs?**
* **Headline:** "KPI Audit."
* **Example:** "Review architecture against the KPI list."
