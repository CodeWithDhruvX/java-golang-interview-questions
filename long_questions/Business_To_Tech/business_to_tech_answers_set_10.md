**Q901: How do you identify the minimal feature set needed to achieve business goals?**
* **Headline:** "User Journey Map."
* **Example:** "Map the critical path. If a feature isn't on the path, it's out."

* **Headline:** "Cost of Delay."
* **Example:** "If we delay X, we lose $10k. If we delay Y, we lose $1k. Do X."

**Q905: How do you measure the success of a product launch beyond adoption metrics?**
* **Headline:** "Satisfaction."
* **Example:** "CSAT/NPS scores."

**Q906: How do you balance long-term vision with short-term customer requests?**
* **Headline:** "Roadmap Tracks."
* **Example:** "Track 1: Strategic (Vision). Track 2: Sustaining (Requests). 70/30 split."

**Q907: How do you manage technical blockers that threaten delivery timelines?**
* **Headline:** "Swarm."
* **Example:** "Whole team focuses on the blocker."

**Q908: How do you determine which experiments are worth scaling into production?**
* **Headline:** "Stat Sig."
* **Example:** "Did the experiment reach statistical significance? Yes? Scale."

**Q909: How do you ensure business outcomes are reflected in product KPIs?**
* **Headline:** "Hierarchy."
* **Example:** "Business KPI -> Product KPI -> Engineering Metric."

**Q910: How do you manage dependencies across multiple products affecting the roadmap?**
* **Headline:** "Program Increments."

**Q911: How do you make product decisions when data and intuition conflict?**
* **Headline:** "Small Bet."
* **Example:** "Intuition says go. Data says stop. Bet small to break the tie."

**Q912: How do you communicate trade-offs and rationale to executive stakeholders?**
* **Headline:** "Narrative."
* **Example:** "Tell the story of the trade-off. Not just the numbers."
