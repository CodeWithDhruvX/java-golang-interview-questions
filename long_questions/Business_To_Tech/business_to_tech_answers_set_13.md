## ðŸ”¹ 1. Technical Product Manager (Questions 1201-1212)

**Q1201: How do you determine which KPIs best reflect the success of a technical feature?**
* **Headline:** "Outcome vs Output."
* **Example:** "Output: Code shipped. Outcome: User saved 5 mins. Measure Outcome."

**Q1209: How do you ensure experimentation results feed directly into product decisions?**
