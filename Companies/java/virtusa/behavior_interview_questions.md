Since you are moving into the **Third Round** for a **Niche QA Test Eng III / Full Stack Developer** role (6+ years experience), the "PM" in this context usually refers to a **Project Manager** or **Portfolio Manager**. 

At Virtusa, this round is a "Managerial Discussion" where they evaluate if you can handle high-level technical delivery, client communication, and team dynamics. They know you can code; now they want to know if you can **lead and deliver.**

Based on your profile and Virtusa’s common patterns, here are the likely questions:

### 1. Delivery & SDLC (The "How" of your 6+ YOE)
* **Agile at Scale:** "With 6 years of experience, you've seen different project phases. Explain how you handle a scenario where the Sprint is ending in 2 days, but a critical 'showstopper' bug is found in the Java backend. How do you communicate this to the client?"
* **Release Management:** "How do you manage code deployments across cross-functional teams? Since you have MySQL and MongoDB experience, how do you handle database migrations without downtime during a production release?"
* **Test-Driven Development (TDD):** "Given the 'Niche QA' aspect of this role, how do you integrate automated testing into the development lifecycle from day one? What metrics do you use to measure code quality?"

### 2. Stakeholder & Team Management
* **Client Communication:** "Virtusa is a service-based company. If a client requests a major feature change in the Angular frontend that wasn't in the original scope, how do you handle that conversation?"
* **Conflict Resolution:** "Tell me about a time you had a technical disagreement with a senior architect or a peer regarding API design (e.g., REST vs. GraphQL). How did you reach a consensus?"
* **Mentorship:** "As a Senior Engineer (Level III), how do you help junior developers improve their 'attention to detail' during code reviews?"

### 3. Technical Strategy (The "Architect" Mindset)
* **Technology Choice:** "Why would you choose Angular for a project over React or Vue? In what scenarios would you recommend MongoDB over MySQL for a client's data needs?"
* **Performance Optimization:** "If a production application is lagging, walk me through your analytical process to find if the bottleneck is in the CSS/JavaScript, the Java backend, or the Database query."
* **Security:** "With your experience in custom APIs, what are the top 3 security measures you always implement to ensure data integrity (e.g., JWT, OAuth, Input Validation)?"

---

### Key Behavioral "Tricky" Questions
* **The Bond/Commitment:** Virtusa often asks about your long-term plans or your comfort with their 2-year bond/agreement policies (common for specific roles).
* **Relocation/Flexibility:** "Are you comfortable working in a hybrid model or relocating if the client project demands it?"
* **The 'Weakness' Question:** "With 6 years of experience, what is one technical area you are currently struggling to master, and how are you addressing it?"

### Preparation Strategy
1.  **Focus on "The Result":** When explaining your past projects, use the **STAR** method (**S**ituation, **T**ask, **A**ction, **R**esult). Quantify your results (e.g., "Reduced page load time by 30%" or "Automated 80% of the regression suite").
2.  **Understand the Business:** Mention how your technical decisions (like choosing Java/MySQL) saved costs or improved time-to-market for the client.
3.  **Attention to Detail:** Since your job description explicitly mentions "Great attention to detail," be ready to talk about a specific time your meticulousness saved a project from a major bug or delay.
